**Headline:**\
Pollerama is a next-generation decentralized social platform built on the Nostr protocol, empowering users to create, share, and interact with content in real-time—without relying on centralized intermediaries.

**Key Features:**

- **Decentralized Network:** Leverage Nostr’s open protocol for censorship-resistant communication, with full control over your data via secure, private key management.
- **Polls & Surveys:** Create dynamic polls, track results in real-time, and analyze engagement with detailed analytics.
- **Content Creation:** Share notes, events, movie ratings, and more with rich media support.
- **Community Moderation:** Customizable moderation tools and user-driven content curation via topics and feeds.
- **Engagement:** Rate events, movies, and profiles; track likes, reposts, and comments.
- **Cross-Platform:** Native Android support and web-ready design for seamless access.
- **Real-Time Interactions:** Live updates and immersive scrolling for seamless user experience.

**For Users:**\
Join a community-driven platform where your voice matters—no censorship, no ads, just pure peer-to-peer interaction.
